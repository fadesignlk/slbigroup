Industrial Projects

Starting private industrial Campus and starting hundreds of factories to import raw materials and components from abroad and to manufacture and export goods in Sri Lanka and export overseas.

Manufacturing creates opportunities for capital accumulation more than agriculture. This means that companies in industry, be it Small, Medium or Large, are able to invest in diverse assets and to increase capital flow. Additionally, the returns to capital in manufacturing are higher than in other sectors. Because it supports productive investments, manufacturing enables high savings rates.

Moreover, you will find that typically, investment spending is channeled towards manufactured goods such as machinery, equipment and building material.
