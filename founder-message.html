Founder Message

S L B I GROUP is not only the number one destination in the world until world destroyed but also on a journey of defining 10 structures to perpetuate a series of community tasks and the company is a journey that brings together all the entrepreneurs to make company employers best the economy.

Our company is ready to promote domestic and foreign manufacturers and market their products globally.  And is to establish branches of our company globally. As a solution to the problem of unemployment in developing countries, it has established vocational colleges in the rural areas of those countries, trained young people and women, set up various manufacturing companies, has produced and marketed internationally and paved the way for solving the problem of unemployment.

We have the opportunity to determine the income of our company employees and help improve their economy. We train everyone who joins our company to do community service and engage in social service work. We provide our customers with the best products and the best market products to suit their cash flow and maintain the best possible relationships with our customers. We will also work to support the economic and socio-economic development of the countries in which our companies operate.

Prof. Rifaitheen Naidu (MBA, PMP, DBA) Founder S L B I Group

S L B I GROUP’s vision is to digitize the Industrial Campus, Courses & Trainings and Manufacturing Products & Services, Export, Import, Supermarkets & Shopping Malls, Wholesale Marketing, Retail Marketing, International Transit and E-Commerce in the world in just a few years and barely.
